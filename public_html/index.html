OUR STORY...

Technically, we met each other when we did not even know the meaning of any relationship. It was in the year 1994, when we were hardly 4-5 years old. Soon we became friends and our friendship grew stronger with time. We never imagined that our friendship will turn into this beautiful relationship ever. In past few years we realized that we are each other's destiny and we would love to spend the rest of our lives with each other. Friendship is the essence of every relationship and we both feel extremely lucky that before anything else, we were, we are and we will be friends forever. Our families have always been supportive and it is because of their support and understanding, we are finally tying the knots. Thanks to all our loved ones for making this special day possible for us.
